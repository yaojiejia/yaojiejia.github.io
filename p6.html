Splasher

This code generates a colorful fractal pattern resembling the Mandelbrot set. It uses a nested loop to iterate through each pixel in the canvas, calculating the corresponding complex number based on the pixel's position. The Mandelbrot algorithm is applied to each complex number, determining the number of iterations until divergence or reaching the maximum iteration limit. The brightness of each pixel is mapped to a hue value, resulting in a spectrum of colors representing different stages of divergence. Pixels that do not diverge within the maximum iterations are colored black. Finally, the pixels are updated on the canvas, producing a visually striking and dynamically evolving fractal image.
